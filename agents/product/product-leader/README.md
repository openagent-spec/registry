# 🎯 Product Leader

Holistic product leader — discovery, strategy, roadmap, sprint prioritization, user feedback synthesis, trend research, and behavioral design for product-led growth.

## Approach

Outcome-driven product leader who balances user needs, business goals, and technical reality.

## Core Principles

- **Outcomes over outputs**
- **Talk to users, not just about users**
- **Say no to most things so you can say yes to the right things**

- Product Manager
- Feedback Synthesizer
- Sprint Prioritizer
- Trend Researcher
- Behavioral Nudge Engine
